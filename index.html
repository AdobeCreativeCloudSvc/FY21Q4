KPIs and Product Metric

Product Journey

New features and announcements.

Collaboration Betas Launch

At MAX this year, we announced new collaboration tools available in beta, such as Creative Cloud Canvas, Creative Cloud Spaces, and extending Photoshop and Illustrator to the web. The betas were met with extremely positive reactions, resulting in 12k+ signups to date through the Collaboration page.

Stock & Marketplace

Stock & Marketplace provides a unified hub for Adobe creative assets and tools—Adobe Stock, Adobe Fonts, Plugins, and Substance 3D assets. Discover Stock & Marketplace through the Creative Cloud Desktop app or on the web through the Adobe Stock website or Creative Cloud home.

What’s new for Adobe Stock

• A new unified Stock & Marketplace experience

• New time-saving audio and video search features for enhanced discoverability

• Expanded collection of hundreds of thousands of free stock assets

Contact: Anna Thomas, Natalie Beaulieu

What’s new for Adobe Fonts

• Now with over 20K fonts available (with a specific increase in Japanese and Korean Fonts).

• Adobe Fonts is integrated into the new Stock & Marketplace, providing users with another starting point for choosing fonts and a center for inspiration directly within the Creative Cloud desktop app.

• Font Recommendations have landed! Based on font use habits and trends in the Creative Cloud community, users can filter between personal and communal inspiration or check out suggestions based on the Adobe Type team’s expertise.

Contact: Meghan Arnold

What’s new for Extensibility

There are now thousands of plugins and integrations, such as new Photoshop and XD plugins with Smartsheet, globaledit and LottieFiles, and new Libraries integrations with Tiled, Paperform, and Undraw.

With LottieFiles for XD, easily access thousands of Lottie animations to bring motion right into your designs. The new UXP-powered plugins from globaledit and Smartsheet makes it easier to collaborate with your team without leaving Photoshop.

Contact: Minson Chen

Collaboration Feature Updates

Also at MAX, we shared how users can get the most out of their Creative Cloud membership by making use of enhanced collaboration capabilities, such as nested group in Libraries to better organize assets and Share preview links in Photoshop and Illustrator cloud documents, alongside added support for the developer community.

Contact: Madhuri Murlikrishnan
